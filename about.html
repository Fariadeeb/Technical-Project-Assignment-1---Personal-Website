About me

I'm Ghifari Adib Ibdafa. I'm a student who is also a novice UI/UX designer. Throughout the lectures, I strengthened and acquired a UI design skills, such as visual design, user research, as well as UX writing skills. My design philosophy is centered around the idea that good design is not just visually pleasing, but also intuitive and easy to use. I strive to create designs that not only look great but also make life easier for the end-user. I am also knowledgeable in front-end development languages such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, which allows me to create designs that are not only visually appealing but also functionally sound.

Interests
